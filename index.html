Horse Box Painting

When your horse box needs painting, how do you choose a company to return it to its original condition? So, what must you look for when selecting a horse box painting company?

  • Can they paint any size of horse box?
  • Do they work on a new build or secondhand box?
  • Can they use your paint-scheme or team colours?
  • Will they help design new paint-schemes and graphics?
  • Do they have sign-writers who can cut and apply silhouettes?

A horse box painting company should cater for older boxes in need of refurbishment. Old panels can be repaired, corroded areas can be replaced, sub-structures can be repaired, and cabs stripped and refurbished.

Professional horse box painting company

A professional horse box painting company should offer all types of paint finishes and use only high-quality products. They should use computerised paint mixing systems like PPG and Glasurit which produce exact colour matches, and their environment should be dust free, so that a high quality finish is achieved.
